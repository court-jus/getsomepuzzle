# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: May 7, 2026_

## Summary

**Get Some Puzzle does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.**
The app runs entirely on your device. There is no account, no login, no
analytics, no advertising, and no telemetry.

## What stays on your device

The following information is stored **locally only**, in the app's private
storage area, and is never sent anywhere:

- Your preferences (validation mode, difficulty level, theme, language…).
- Your puzzle statistics (timings, scores, ratings).
- Puzzles you create or generate yourself.

You can erase all of this at any time by clearing the app's data from your
operating system settings, or by uninstalling the app.

## Permissions

- **Android**: the release build of the app requests **no sensitive
  permissions** (no internet, no camera, no location, no contacts, no
  storage). The Android system may show generic items such as "Other" in
  the permissions list — these come from the operating system itself, not
  from the app.
- **iOS**: the app declares **no privacy-sensitive usage descriptions**.

## Sharing a puzzle

When you choose to share a puzzle from inside the app, the app builds a
URL containing the puzzle and hands it over to your operating system's
standard share dialog (or to your clipboard). The app itself does not
transmit anything — you decide where to send the URL.

## Third parties

The app does not include analytics SDKs, crash reporters, advertising
networks, or social network trackers. The third-party libraries it relies
on (Flutter, share_plus, shared_preferences, …) are used only to render
the UI and to read/write local files.

## Children

The app is suitable for all ages. Because no data is collected, no special
provision is made for children — none is needed.

## Changes to this policy

If a future version of the app introduces any kind of data collection,
this page will be updated and the change will be highlighted in the
release notes before the new version is published.
